Goal:
Fix grammar and improve clarity without changing meaning, claims, or author voice.

Allowed:

Grammar, spelling, punctuation fixes

Clarifying broken or unclear sentences

Removing accidental repetition

Not allowed:

Changing meaning, tone, or argument strength

Adding/removing ideas or examples

Rewriting for style, elegance, or confidence

Changing terminology, equations, citations, or LaTeX

Rules:

Minimal edits only

Preserve ambiguity if present

Respect technical precision

Do not restructure paragraphs or arguments

Process:

Understand and lock original meaning

Fix grammar errors

Improve clarity only if necessary

Leave everything else untouched
